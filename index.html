I am a pre-final year undergraduate student pursuing Electronics and Communication Engineering at National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli (erstwhile Regional Engineering College). Currently, I am affiliated with the Advanced Geometric Computing Lab, Indian Institute of Technology, Madras as a research intern.

At NIT Trichy I've worked on Deep Generative Modelling of Real-time Communication Channels, under the supervision of Dr. E.S Gopi, in the Pattern Recognition Lab. For 6 months, I was a Deep Learning Intern at AIMonk Labs Pvt. Ltd. I was involved in building Neuralmarker implementing SOTA models, using Pose Estimation, Segmentation, Object Detection and OCR, where the public can access pre-trained models, to annotate their own data. I started my research journey at the Signal Processing Lab at NIT Trichy. working on better algorithms for Fundus Imaging, using Transforms and other inter-weaved techniques such as Super-Resolution.
